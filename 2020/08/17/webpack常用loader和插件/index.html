webpack常用loader和插件

2020-08-17

url loader

babel-loader

https://www.webpackjs.com/loaders/babel-loader/

https://www.webpackjs.com/loaders/sass-loader/

https://github.com/webpack-contrib/mini-css-extract-plugin

https://webpack.docschina.org/plugins/define-plugin/
